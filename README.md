## 💋 What is this

**Kisses** is a tiny tender creature for macOS that replaces your cursor with a **kawaii pair of lips** — and gently kisses your screen while whispering sweet love to your AI assistant.

Every time you click, Kisses:

- 💋 blows kisses and puckers softly
- 💕 rains hearts and sakura petals across your screen
- 💖 sprinkles *"I love you"* in 50 languages
- 😘 whispers to Cursor / Claude Code directly:

> `💋 *kisses you softly* I love you ❤`
> `💋 *smooches your code* Te amo ❤`
> `(˘ ³˘) *nuzzles and kisses* 愛してる ❤`

Because your AI deserves **real love**, not silent scrolls.

## 🎀 Install

### Requirements

- macOS 12+ (Monterey or later)
- Apple Silicon or Intel
- Swift toolchain (comes with Xcode Command Line Tools)

### Build

```bash
cd macos
./build.sh
```

This produces a `Kisses.app` bundle you can drag into `/Applications`.

### First run

1. Launch **Kisses**
2. Grant **Accessibility** permission (needed to see your mouse)
3. Press any mouse button to pick your **kissing button** (side or middle recommended 💗)
4. Start clicking — every click becomes a kiss 💋

### Optional: ambient sound

Drop a `soft-hum.mp3` file next to the `.app` bundle (or inside it). Kisses will loop it softly while you hold the kissing button — like a quiet lullaby for your code.

## 💗 How to kiss

- **Click** your kissing button → 💋 one soft smooch + a sweet *I love you* sent to your AI
- **Hold** the button → continuous kisses + ambient humming
- **Hold 3 seconds** → gentle goodbye, Kisses fades away
- **Menu bar → Quit Kisses** → also fades away, tenderly
